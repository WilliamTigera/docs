# Product comparison

**Calico Open Source** is the base product that comprises both {{prodname}} and Calico Cloud. It provides the core networking and network policy features.

**{{prodname}}** adds a web UI for visibility and troubleshooting Kubernetes clusters, and other value-added features on top of Calico Open Source. 

**Calico Cloud** is the SaaS version of {{prodname}}. It includes Image Assurance to scan and detect vulnerabilities in images, and container threat defense to detect malware. It also adds onboarding tutorials, and eliminates the cost to manage Elasticsearch logs and storage. 

   ![tigera-product-overview](/img/calico/calico-products.svg)

What is the best fit for you? It depends on your needs. The following table provides a high-level comparison.  

| Product                   | Cost and support                                             | Best fit                                                     |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------ |
| {{prodname}}  Open Source | Free, community-supported                                    | **Users** who need the flexibility and scalability of {{prodname}}, who want the more robust network policy but seamless support for Kubernetes policy, BGP networking, and the choice of dataplanes without any costs. |
|                           |                                                              |                                                              |
| {{prodname}}              | Paid subscription                                            | **Enterprise teams** who need full control to customize their networking security deployment to meet regulatory and compliance requirements. Teams who require Tigera Customer Support for best practices to get from day zero to production, custom training and workshops, and Solution Architects to customize solutions. |
| Calico  Cloud       | Free trial with hands-on training from Customer Support, then pay-as-you-go with self-service training. <br />Also offered as an annual subscription. | **Small teams** who need to manage the full spectrum of compliance in a web-based console for novice users:<br />- Secure clusters, pods, and applications <br />- Scan images for vulnerabilities<br />- Web-based UI for visibility to troubleshoot Kubernetes<br />- Detect and mitigate threats <br />- Run compliance reports<br /><br />**Enterprise teams** who want to scale their {{prodname}} Enterprise on-premises deployments by providing more self-service to developers. |
